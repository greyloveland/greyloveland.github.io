About

Grey Tiger Technology LLC is a solo data analytics and IT consulting firm based in Saint George, Utah. Founded by Greyson, a CASS Supervisor at SkyWest Airlines with direct experience building and presenting Power BI dashboards to FAA oversight personnel.

Analytical work spans aviation safety, regulatory compliance reporting, and DOD-adjacent data operations. Grey Tiger is positioned to support government subcontracting and prime contracting opportunities where rigorous data work and federal domain knowledge are required.

Active SAM.gov registrant. All project work uses publicly available federal datasets and is fully documented for review.
